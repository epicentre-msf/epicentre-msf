## About Us

Epicentre was founded in 1987 to strengthen the scientific basis of MSF's medical and humanitarian interventions. Our work focuses on:

- **Epidemiological surveillance and research** in humanitarian crisis settings
- **Outbreak investigation and response** to infectious disease emergencies
- **Operational research** to improve medical practices and healthcare delivery
- **Training and capacity building** in epidemiology and research methods

## Our Work

We conduct studies and research projects across various domains:

- Infectious diseases (malaria, measles, cholera, meningitis, etc.)
- Vaccination strategies and effectiveness
- Nutrition and food security
- Non-communicable diseases in humanitarian contexts
- Mental health
- Access to healthcare

## Open Source Commitment

This GitHub organization hosts our open-source projects, data analysis tools, and research code to promote transparency and reproducibility in humanitarian research.
